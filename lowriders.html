Thank You for your interest in entering your car/bike into our 6th Annual Barrio Muertos Party/Car Show. We are doing things a little different this year in order to help us better control the flow of traffic and parking situation. We have 50 available car show spots and 20 bike spots. We are giving PRIORITY to cars and bikes wanting to enter the trunk decorating contest and those wanting to enter to compete for People’s Choice & Best Paint Job. With that being said, the reserved spots are strictly for the car show entrees.

Car Entree: $20

Bike Entree: $15

Awards are as follows:

Car Ofrenda Contest

  • 1st Place: $100, 1st Place Plaque
  • 2nd Place: 2nd Place Plaque
  • 3rd Place: 2nd Place Plaque

People's Choice Car

  • 1st Place Plaque

Best Paint Job

  • 1st Place Plaque
  • 2st Place Plaque
  • 3st Place Plaque

Bike Ofrenda Contest

  • 1st Place: $100, 1st Place Plaque
  • 2nd Place: 2nd Place Plaque
  • 3rd Place: 2nd Place Plaque

People's Choice Bike

  • 1st Place Plaque

Please only register below if you plan on entering your car to decorate your trunk or to compete for People’s Choice or best paint job. (If entering for People’s Choice or best paint job, trunk decoration is not required but is an added plus!)

After you register, someone from our team will reach out within a few days to confirm and send an invoice to reserve your place.

**If you do not plan on entering your show car but would still like to join us, public parking will be available (separate from car show section) on a first come - first serve basis. LETS KEEP THE DRAMA AT HOME
