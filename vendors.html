VENDORS

Thank you for your interest in the 2022 Barrio Muertos Party. We hope to include a variety of vendors, featuring handmade goods, apparel, jewelry, etc. The event is on November 5, 2022 at Alamo Beer from 5pm - 12pm. Vendor setup begins at 3pm. The vendor fee is $100.

Space is limited and vendor applications are reviewed on a first-come/first serve basis. Signing up does not guarantee you will be selected. If you are selected, you will receive a confirmation email. Your spot will be confirmed once your non-refundable payment is submitted. Your payment is due within 5 business days.

Please review the following guidelines:

  1. Barrio Muertos will provide one 10x10 space. Vendors are required to provide their own setup materials, such as a canopy, lights, tables, electrical cords, etc
  2. Vendors must keep all products in their assigned space
  3. Merchandise categories/types must be included in the application. We do not want multiple vendors selling the same items.
  4. Each vendor is responsible for cleaning up their area
  5. Alamo Beer and selected food vendors will provide all alcoholic, food, and beverage sales throughout the event. Vendors are not allowed to sell any of these types of items
  6. Vendors are responsible for the security of their cash, inventory, supplies, etc
  7. Only submit an application if you agree to the guidelines
