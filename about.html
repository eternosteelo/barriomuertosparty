ABOUT

We are excited to announce the 5th annual Barrio Muertos Cultural Celebration, honoring our Dia de Muertos traditions with an urban twist.

Come and enjoy a memorable evening where our ancestors visit us and experience all we have to offer. There will be altares displayed in the trunks of lowrider cars, an art exhibit by some of the most talented creatives in the scene, photo ops, DJs spinning the best of 90's hip hop on vinyl & digital format, local vendors, food vendors, an Aztec ceremony blessing our event, a break dance showcase, and facepaint/crafting activities for the kids.

Experience a FREE and vibrant celebration for all ages on November 4, 2023, at Alamo Beer from 5 pm - 12am. Check out our 2022 recap below!

WHO

Presented by Eterno Steelo clothing brand, established in 2013, by David Espinoza aka Davy. Davy has built a loyal following from years of networking through the brand and curating events that bring together the elements of the barrio/hispanic culture. Our brand represents familia, street Culture & the hustle of our people, which is highlighted through our apparel and events.
