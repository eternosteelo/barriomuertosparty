ABOUT

The 4th annual Barrio Muertos Cultural Celebration, honoring our Dia de Muertos traditions with an urban twist. Be part of a memorable evening in which our ancesotors visit us and enjoy the altares being displayed in the trunk of lowrider automobiles, an art exhibit by some of the most talented creatives in the scene, photo opps. Experience a vibrant celebration for all ages with loteria for the familia, face painting, and other activities.

WHO

Presented by Eterno Steelo clothing brand, the 4th annual event seeks support and sponsorship in order to reach a larger audience and grow attendance. Our social media outreach is over 18,000 with a demographic between 24-40. Our events reach a demographic of 40-60 year olds, which demonstrates a wide reach of the population. Counting on your support, we hope to reach a larger audience and have Barrio Muertos become a standard of cultural excellence in the San Antonio community.
